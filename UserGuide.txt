User Guide: Getting Started


Features:

Tasks
1. Adding tasks to the list! Press the add button to add a new task. 
2. Fill in the fields for location, times, and categories.
3. Go about your daily life and chYour will help you find time to fit these tasks into your schedule.

Appointments
1. As you are adding your task to the list, you must select a category.
2. For the category option, choose “Appointments”.
3. Make sure you enter a correct and accurate value for location and time.

Adding a Calendar
1. Go to the settings menu.
2. Link either your Google Calendar or Outlook Calendar by pressing their respective buttons.
3. Log in to your accounts and they will be updated on chYour!

Adding Family Members
1. Go to the settings menu.
2. Click Add Family Members
3. Search for them via email or phone number.

Deleting Account
1. Go to the settings menu.
2. Click on deactivate account
3. Confirm that you will be leaving chYour.
